\theorem[3.1 Riemann's theorem on removable singularities]{f hol on $\Omega-\{z_0\}$, if f is bounded on $\Omega-\{z_0\}$,then $z_0$ is a removable singularity}
\corollary[3.2]{Suppose that f has an isolated singularity at the point $z_0$, Then $z_0$ is a pole $\iff$ $|f(z)\to\infty|$ as $z\to z_0$}
\vspace*{10pt}

Isolated singularities belong to one of three categories:\par
1.Removable singularities (f bounded near $z_0$)\par
2.Pole singularities ($|f(z_0)\to\infty\;as\;z\to z_0|$)\par
3.Essential singularities\\
